### Week 9 Achievements

* Gained a solid understanding of **AWS Service Quotas** and the importance of service limits in managing AWS resources.

* Learned how to access and use the **AWS Service Quotas Console** to monitor and manage account quotas.

* Successfully customized the Service Quotas Dashboard to display frequently monitored AWS service quotas.

* Successfully submitted a quota increase request for **Amazon EC2 Running On-Demand Standard Instances**, increasing the quota from **32** to **100** instances.

* Learned how to submit and track quota increase requests using the **Quota Request History** feature in the AWS Console.

* Understood the meaning of different quota request statuses, including:
  * **Closed**
  * **Quota request approved**
  * **Quota requested**

* Learned how to monitor request progress through AWS email notifications and review detailed request information from the AWS Console.

* Gained an understanding of how **AWS Support** reviews, approves, or rejects Service Quota increase requests.

* Successfully completed the hands-on lab and gained practical experience in managing AWS Service Quotas to support future infrastructure scaling requirements.
