During my **12-week internship and training program** in the **First Cloud AI Journey (Workforce Bootcamp)** at **Amazon Web Services (AWS) Vietnam**, I had the opportunity to learn, collaborate, and gain valuable hands-on experience with cloud technologies. The following are my personal reflections and suggestions to help improve the program for future participants.

## Overall Evaluation

### 1. Working Environment

The learning and working environment at AWS Vietnam was professional, collaborative, and highly motivating. The open workspace encouraged teamwork, knowledge sharing, and creativity, allowing interns to collaborate effectively on technical projects.

### 2. Mentor and Team Support

The mentors were knowledgeable, approachable, and always willing to provide guidance whenever needed. Instead of simply giving answers, they encouraged problem-solving and independent thinking. The program administrators were also responsive and provided sufficient learning resources and AWS credits throughout the internship.

### 3. Relevance to My Major

The internship aligned well with my academic background in Information Technology and Cloud Computing. It allowed me to apply theoretical knowledge to real-world scenarios while gaining practical experience with AWS services and modern cloud architectures.

### 4. Learning and Skill Development

The program significantly improved both my technical and professional skills. I gained hands-on experience with AWS Serverless technologies, backend development using Node.js, REST API development, database management, system deployment, testing, and technical documentation. I also strengthened my teamwork, communication, and presentation skills through regular project discussions and demonstrations.

### 5. Company Culture and Teamwork

AWS promotes a collaborative and supportive working culture where everyone is encouraged to contribute ideas and continuously learn. Working with mentors and fellow interns made the learning experience enjoyable and helped me improve my collaboration skills.

### 6. Internship Benefits

Although the program was primarily a training bootcamp, it provided valuable learning opportunities through access to AWS accounts, cloud resources, technical documentation, and hands-on labs. These resources enabled me to experiment with real cloud infrastructure and gain practical experience beyond classroom learning.

---

## Additional Questions

### What satisfied you the most during the internship?

The most valuable aspect of the internship was the opportunity to build and deploy a complete **AWS Serverless Event-Driven application** using real AWS services. Working on a practical project significantly enhanced my understanding of cloud architecture and modern software development practices.

### What do you think could be improved for future interns?

The program could consider allocating additional time for the final project, allowing participants to further optimize their solutions, perform more comprehensive testing, and explore advanced AWS services.

### Would you recommend this internship to your friends? Why?

Yes. I would highly recommend this internship to students interested in Cloud Computing and Software Engineering. The program provides practical experience, guidance from AWS professionals, and valuable opportunities to develop both technical and professional skills.

## Suggestions and Future Expectations

### Suggestions

- Organize more advanced technical workshops on topics such as DevOps, Infrastructure as Code (IaC), Cloud Security, and AI applications on AWS.
- Provide additional sessions focused on AWS certification preparation to help participants pursue professional certifications.

### Future Expectations

I hope to continue learning and developing my expertise in AWS Cloud technologies and pursue a career as a **Cloud Engineer** or **Cloud Solutions Architect** in the future. I also look forward to contributing to the AWS community through continuous learning and practical cloud projects.
